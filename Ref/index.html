Seated Sciatic Nerve Floss

  1. Sit upright with feet flat on the floor
  2. Extend one leg straight while pointing toes away
  3. At the same time, bend your head down (chin to chest)
  4. Reverse: flex foot toward you, lift head up
  5. Repeat gently 10-15 times, then switch legs
